Our Story

Black Market means black shop and its name originated in the 19th
century. At that time, a large number of Chinese migrated to Malaysia,
and Gan Chan Heng, a businessman at the time, founded the first
Black Market in Kuala Lumpur, the capital of Malaysia. The inspiration
for the name was the intention to cover the store with a mysterious freshness.


The original intention of Black Market was to create trendy and innovative
clothing for young people. The original intention at that time is still in use
today, and the founders continue to promote new policies to adapt to the changes
of the times. Until the 20th century, we still maintained stable development
because we continued to update the decoration and clothing styles in the store
following the policy of the founder. This is also thanks to our in-store designer
Chin Chi Yan and costume designer Chua Jing Wei.

Black Market is not just a clothing store, Black Market is also a symbol
of the history and passion for clothing.
