BlacK MarkeT

Crossover is a premium streetwear store, established in 2005. A true believer in individualism, BlacK MarkeT strive to breathe in new culture and to bring together people with passion for true self-independent and self-expression.
The man behind BlacK MarkeT is a man in constant search for authentic and exclusive apparel, streetwear brands.
Each brand within BlacK MarkeT is carefully sourced and selected, to ensure it represent values that are parallel to BlacK MarkeT core principles.
From a humble beginning, BlacK MarkeT begun to establish itself as to shop at for hard-to-find brands and rising streetwear names. Our goal is to not only be a leader in streetwear retail / distribution industry in Southeast Asia, but also to lead the innovation of premium goods and services. Our vision is to bring the world closer to our customers.
